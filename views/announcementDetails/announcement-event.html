FTVE Skills Competition 2026

December 28, 2025 FTVE Office

The Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FTVE) proudly announces the FTVE Skills Competition 2026, an annual event designed to showcase the technical expertise, creativity, and problem-solving abilities of FTVE students across various disciplines.

The competition aims to promote excellence in technical and vocational education by providing students with an opportunity to apply their classroom knowledge in practical, real-world scenarios while fostering teamwork, discipline, and professional confidence.

Event Highlights

  • Participants: Selected students from FTVE programs
  • Categories: Skills-based challenges aligned with technical and vocational fields
  • Evaluation: Performance will be assessed by qualified faculty members and invited industry practitioners

Guidelines and Reminders

Participants are expected to observe proper decorum, comply with event rules, and follow safety protocols throughout the competition. Specific mechanics and schedules will be provided to registered participants prior to the event.

Non-participating students and faculty members are encouraged to attend and support the competitors as they demonstrate their skills and dedication to their chosen fields.

Further details, including the official program, competition mechanics, and venue assignments, will be announced through official FTVE communication channels. For inquiries, please coordinate with the FTVE Office.
