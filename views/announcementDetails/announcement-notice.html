Notice

Submission of Thesis Project Requirements

December 20, 2025 FTVE Office

The Faculty of Technical and Vocational Education (FTVE) hereby informs all graduating students that the submission of thesis project requirements for the current academic term will commence on the indicated date.

Students are required to complete and submit all necessary documents in accordance with the guidelines set by their respective departments. Only submissions that fully comply with the prescribed format and requirements will be accepted for evaluation.

Required Documents

  • Approved thesis manuscript endorsed by the adviser
  • Signed approval and endorsement forms
  • Final presentation slides and supporting materials (if applicable)

Important Reminders

Students are strongly advised to coordinate closely with their thesis advisers prior to submission to ensure completeness and accuracy of all requirements. Late or incomplete submissions may result in delays in evaluation and graduation processing.

All thesis project requirements must be submitted to the FTVE Office within official office hours. Specific deadlines, submission procedures, and additional instructions will be communicated by the respective departments.

For questions or clarifications, students may contact the FTVE Office or their assigned thesis advisers during office hours.
