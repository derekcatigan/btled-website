About FETI

Learn about the history, mission, vision, and academic strengths of the Faculty of Education Technology and Innovation.

Our History

The Faculty of Education Technology and Innovation (FETI) of Southern Leyte State University was established to address the growing demand for competent educators and professionals in technical, vocational, and livelihood education.

Over the years, FETI has continuously evolved its curriculum to align with national standards, industry needs, and community development initiatives—producing graduates who are skilled, ethical, and service-oriented.

Our Mission

To provide quality instruction, innovative training, and community- responsive programs that develop competent and globally competitive technical and vocational educators.

Our Vision

A leading faculty in technical and vocational education, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and meaningful community impact.

Why Enroll in FETI?

FETI offers a dynamic learning environment that prepares students for professional teaching careers and industry-based opportunities.

Industry-Relevant Curriculum

Community & Career Impact

Graduates contribute to education, industry, and community development.
