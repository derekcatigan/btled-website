How to Apply

Six Steps to Enrollment

1

Online Application

Submit your requirements via the SLSU portal at my.southernleytestateu.edu.ph.

2

Receive Application Feedback

Wait for a confirmation or follow-up from the Admissions Office regarding your submitted documents.

3

Take the SLSU Entrance Exam

Attend the scheduled SLSU Entrance Examination (SEE) at the designated venue and time.

4

Receive SEE Results

Your examination results will be released through the official SLSU communication channels.

5

Program-Based Assessment

Undergo the required interview, skills test, or agility test specific to your chosen program.

6

Enrollment Eligibility Notice

Receive your official notification confirming your eligibility to enroll at SLSU.

Step 1 — Documents Needed

Admission Requirements

Required for All Applicants

  • NSO / PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2×2 photo with white background and nametag
  • Certificate of Employment of Parent (if SLSU dependent)

Senior High School Graduate

  • NSO / PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2×2 photo with white background and nametag
  • Grade 11 Grades (Report Card)
  • Grade 12 Certificate of Enrollment, signed by the School Principal

Alternative Learning System Passer

  • NSO / PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2×2 photo with white background and nametag
  • Certificate of Rating, signed by the School Principal
  • Certificate of Eligibility for Admission to College

Non-SHS Graduate

  • NSO / PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2×2 photo with white background and nametag
  • Form 137 — Permanent Record

Transferee

  • NSO / PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2×2 photo with white background and nametag
  • Transcript of Records from previous school

Special Provisions

Guaranteed Admission Categories

Person with Disability (PWD)

  • PWD ID
  • Fit to Study Certification from a licensed Physician

ALS Passers

  • Certificate of Eligibility to Enroll in College

Indigenous People (IP)

  • IP ID or Barangay Certificate

Dependents of SLSU Regular Employees

  • Birth Certificate or Marriage Certificate
  • Certificate of Employment from SLSU HRMO

Sports, Culture & Arts Winners

  • Certificate of Awards (Regional or National)
  • For team sports: Certification of merit from SLSU coaches/trainers after official try-outs

Solo Parent or Child of Solo Parent

  • Solo Parent ID

Victims of Conflict & Displacement

  • DSWD Certification as internally displaced person

Dependents of E-CLIP Enrollees

  • Certificate from DILG under the Enhanced Comprehensive Local Integration Program

Orphans

  • Certification from DSWD

First Generation College Students

  • Barangay Certificate subject to verification by the Admission Committee
