Education Program

Industrial Arts

Develops hands-on competencies in woodworking, welding, drafting, and technical trades for real-world applications.

Program Overview

Back

Industrial Arts is a technical–vocational program that develops students’ competencies in trade and technical skills such as woodworking, welding, drafting, electrical installation, and machinery operations. The program emphasizes hands-on training, problem-solving, and safety, preparing learners for employment, entrepreneurship, or further studies in industrial and technical fields.
