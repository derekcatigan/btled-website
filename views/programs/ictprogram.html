Education Program

Information & Communication Technology (ICT)

Focuses on computer systems, basic programming, digital tools, and technology-related skills.

Program Overview

Back

The ICT major is designed to produce teachers who are not only "tech-savvy" but are also experts in Instructional Design. Students learn to master computer systems, networking, and software development, and then apply that knowledge to create effective learning environments.

Technical Laboratory Training

Hands-on Teaching Workshop

Course Curriculum

Year Core Focus Key Subjects
1st Year Digital Literacy Computer hardware, office productivity tools, and ICT fundamentals.
2nd Year Technical Core Database management, system analysis, and advanced programming.
3rd Year Pedagogical Integration Methods of teaching ICT, assessment in tech-driven classes, and networking.
4th Year Professional Immersion Capstone projects (system development) and actual practice teaching.
