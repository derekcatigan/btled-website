Education Program

Agriculture & Fishery Arts

Prepares students with hands-on knowledge in farming, aquaculture, and modern agricultural practices.

Program Overview

Back

Agriculture & Fishery Arts is a technical–vocational track that equips students with practical knowledge and hands-on skills in farming, animal production, and fisheries. The program focuses on sustainable agricultural practices, food production, and the use of modern tools and techniques to support local and national food systems. It prepares learners for entrepreneurship, employment in agri-based industries, or further studies in agriculture and fisheries.
