Faculty of Technology and Vocational Education

The Faculty of Technology and Vocational Education (FTVE) of Southern Leyte State University – Main Campus is dedicated to preparing future educators and professionals in technical, vocational, and livelihood education.

FTVE offers specialized programs designed to prepare competent educators and professionals.

WHY CHOOSE FTVE?

Modern facilities, expert faculty, and strong community engagement.

MODERN FACILITIES

Our campus features state-of-the-art laboratories, workshops, and learning spaces equipped with modern technology. These facilities are designed to support hands-on learning, research, and innovation in a dynamic academic environment.

EXPERT FACULTY

Our faculty members are highly qualified professionals with strong academic backgrounds and industry experience. They are committed to delivering quality education, mentoring students, and fostering critical thinking, innovation, and lifelong learning.

STUDENT ACHIEVEMENTS

Our students consistently demonstrate excellence through academic competitions, innovative research, and impactful projects. Their accomplishments reflect strong technical skills, creativity, and dedication recognized at institutional, national, and international levels.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

Actively promotes social responsibility through meaningful participation in community outreach, service-learning programs, and local initiatives. These engagements strengthen partnerships, foster civic awareness, and encourage students to apply knowledge for the greater good.
